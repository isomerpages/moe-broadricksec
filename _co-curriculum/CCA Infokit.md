<h4><strong>Co-curricular Activities (CCA)</strong></h4>
<h4>Purpose</h4>
<p>Co-Curricular Activities (CCA) at Broadrick focuses on facilitating experiential
learning outside of the classroom. Through CCAs, we allow students to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Discover interests and talents</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop character, values and skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Learn and develop leadership and teamwork</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrate a spirit of service</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Form friendships and deepen sense of belonging to the school and community</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h4>Schedule</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Monday </strong>- 3.15pm to 6.00pm</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Wednesday </strong>- 3.15pm to 6.00pm</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Friday- 2pm to 5.30pm (<em>only if necessary, e.g. competition</em>)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>CCA teachers will inform your child of the CCA schedule.</p>
<h4>Requirements</h4>
<p>Students must achieve the minimum 75% attendance requirement.</p>
