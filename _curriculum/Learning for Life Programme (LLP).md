<h4><strong>Learning for Life</strong></h4>
<h4><strong>Our Objectives</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>
<p>To nurture Grateful, Respectful, and Compassionate (GRC) Leaders through
service in the&nbsp; community. It takes reference from our school’s GRC
Values and is aligned to the school’s mission of nurturing Broadricians
to be future-ready learners with strength of character.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Our Programmes</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Sec 1 - “Adopt A Hood” Project in collaboration with Dunman High School
to clean the neighbourhood.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sec 2 - Students host their young counterparts from Kong Hwa School.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sec 3 - Hearty Bites Programme where students engage elderly in a variety
of activities and serve them meals.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
