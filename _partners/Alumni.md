<p>Established in 1974, the Broadrick Alumni (BA) serves as a meaningful
platform for former students to stay connected with their alma mater and
fellow schoolmates. The association organises a variety of engaging activities,
including the annual Lunar New Year Celebration, Mid-Autumn Reunion, and
Durian Fiesta. Members also enjoy monthly karaoke sessions, as well as
other recreational activities such as &nbsp;line dancing, and overseas
trips.</p>
<p>The BA continues to collaborate closely with the school, supporting and
participating in various programmes that connect alumni with current Broadricians.
Through these meaningful engagements, members express their gratitude for
the experiences and values gained during their student days, giving back
to the school community that once nurtured them.</p>
<p>Join us to relive cherished memories, reconnect with friends, and continue
contributing to the Broadrick spirit! &nbsp;<a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/broadrickalumni" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Click here!</a>
</p>
