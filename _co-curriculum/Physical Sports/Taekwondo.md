<h3><strong>Objectives</strong></h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop physical fitness, mental discipline, and strong moral character
in our members.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To cultivate resilience in both training and everyday life.</p>

<li>

in the sport.</p>
