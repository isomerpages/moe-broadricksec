<p><strong>Demonstrating Gratitude, Respect, and Compassion through Overseas VIA:</strong>
</p>
<p>Students will:</p>
<p>✅ <strong>Engage in community service and collaborate with ASEAN communities</strong> to
foster global awareness and social responsibility.</p>
<p>✅ <strong>Experience different cultural norms, traditions, and languages</strong> to
enhance their understanding of ASEAN identities and interconnectedness.</p>
<p>✅ <strong>Adapt to real-world challenges in overseas settings</strong>,
developing problem-solving skills, resourcefulness, and resilience.</p>
<p>✅ <strong>Enhance active listening, empathy, and respect</strong> when navigating
cross-cultural interactions.</p>
<p>✅ <strong>Develop adaptability and leadership confidence</strong> through
exposure to diverse working styles in unfamiliar environments.</p>
