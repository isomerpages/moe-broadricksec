<h3><strong>Objectives</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>
<p>To foster analytical thinking and creativity, with opportunities to explore
educational robotics through collaborative challenge-based exercises.</p>

teamwork.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
